Ontology of satisfaction surveys about flight services: Description back to ToC

The ontology of flight service satisfaction surveys aims to represent the knowledge in a structured way of passengers' opinions, assessments and ratings of the services offered by airlines, such as: seat comfort, quality of food and beverage service, customer service, etc.
